Delete Your Game Data - Brick Breaker Max

If you want to reset your progress, coins, gems, or settings, follow these steps:

Android Devices:

  • Open Settings on your device.
  • Go to Apps or Application Manager.
  • Find and select Brick Breaker Max.
  • Tap Storage.
  • Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage to delete all saved progress.

After deletion, the game will start fresh as if you installed it for the first time.
